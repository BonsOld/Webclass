Project 1 | Website Critiques | Benson Young

Questions:

  1. What was your first impression of the site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information? (Do they have links on the left and text on the right? Some other scheme? No scheme?)
  3. Does the site structure make sense or did it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If a background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was the navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

Answers:

https://utah.com/national-parks
  1. My first impression of the website was that it is very clean cut.
  2. The website has pictures that are linked all in a bundle. It also has a dropdown navbar at the top.
  3. The site structure makes sense to me because all of the links are organized the same way.
  4. The graphics and layout are helpful. When you scroll down, it creates a helpful button to take you back to the top.
  5. The website didn't take super long to load, it took about 2 seconds.
  6. The website had a background picture for the main item, and it added to the site.
  7. The website had a navbar that was very helpful in finding information.
  8. The site had a look and feel that was the same in the different pages.
  9. The site performed perfectly with smaller windows.
  10. The site offered a button that takes you back to the top, which is very nice for long pages.
https://pitchfork.com
  1. My first impression of the website is that it has a minimalist design.
  2. The website organizes information with a navbar and scrolling down.
  3. The site's organization doesn't make sense to me because the headings are small and easy to miss.
  4. The multimedia adds to the site because you get to see what the link is like before you click on it.
  5. The homepage was pretty fast to load.
  6. The text color and size could've been better. The headings were really small, and some of the words blended in with the picture.
  7. The site had a sticky navbar, which was nice.
  8. The look of the pages were similar to each other, but not to the homepage.
  9. The window size responsiveness worked really well for smaller pages.
  10. I think that the sticky navbar is a nice touch.
https://sabletine.com
  1. My first impression of the site is that it is for a small company.
  2. The website has information organized in a navbar.
  3. The site structure was very minimalist, and not confusing.
  4. The graphics layout is nice, I like the addition of the tweet window.
  5. The homepage was very quick to load.
  6. The text color and size didn't disrupt the page while reading.
  7. The page had a horizontal navbar that lead to over similar looking pages.
  8. The white color theme was the same throughout the pages.
  9. The site didn't respond at all when I changed the window size.
  10. The site offered a tweets mini-window that showed recent tweets they made.
